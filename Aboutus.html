About Us

Our founding principles

Ever since our founding in 2005 The Laurier Barbershop has been a staple of the Sandy Hill community. Whether it be for a job interview, to prepare for a big date or to simply cut long hair our Barbershop has had the privilege to serve our customers and to prepare them for their life ahead. Our founder, Jeremy Tremblay, is an Ottawa native and an alumni of the University of Ottawa who has deep ties to the city. He quit his job as a business analyst for Transport Canada in Calgary to pursue his dream of operating a Barbershop in the city of his youth. Come in any time we are open for a haricut and convesation. We Pride ourselves on our excellent service and we hope to see and serve you as best we can.
